About aIQversity

Founded by women entrepreneurs, building AI-driven education for underserved regions and empowering the next generation

A Vision by Women Entrepreneurs

Founded by passionate women entrepreneurs, aIQversity represents the power of diverse leadership in technology and education. Our mission is to make AI-driven, personalized learning accessible to every learner, especially those from underserved regions, non-engineering backgrounds, and Tier 2/3 cities.

We believe that women-led innovation is vital to building a more equitable and empowered India. Through our platform, we aim to bridge the learning gap by offering hyper-personalized study experiences, AI-powered tutoring, and real-time feedback that adapts to every student's unique needs.
