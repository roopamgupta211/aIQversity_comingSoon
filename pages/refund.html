Secure Refund Process

Important Notice

Effective Date: 15/01/2025
Last Updated: 15/01/2025

At aIQversity, we strive to provide the best AI-powered learning experience to our users. We understand that sometimes you may need to request a refund. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued for subscriptions, courses, and other paid services.

Quick Refund Overview

📅

Monthly Plans

No refunds after payment. Cancel anytime to stop future charges.

🗓️

Annual Plans

Full refund within 7 days of purchase. No refunds after.

📚

Course Purchases

Refund within 3 days if less than 10% accessed.

1

Eligibility for Refunds

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

A. Subscription Plans

📌

Monthly Subscription

  • • Refunds are not available for monthly plans after payment.
  • • You can cancel anytime to prevent future charges.
📌

Annual Subscription

  • • If you cancel within 7 days of purchase, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • • After 7 days, no refunds will be processed, but you can continue to use the service until the subscription expires.

B. One-Time Course Purchases

📌

Refunds are available within 3 days of course purchase ONLY if:

  • • You have not accessed more than 10% of the course content.
  • • You have not downloaded any course materials.
📌

No refunds for:

❌ Courses already completed
❌ Downloaded course materials

C. Paid Mock Tests & AI-Generated Question Papers

📌

No refunds for one-time mock test purchases or AI-generated question papers, as these are digital services provided instantly.

D. Technical Issues

📌

If you experience a technical issue preventing access, you must:

  • • Report the issue to support@aiqversity.com within 48 hours of purchase.
  • • If the issue is not resolved within 7 days, a full refund will be provided.
2

How to Request a Refund?

📌

Step 1

Email support@aiqversity.com with:

  • • Order ID & Payment Receipt
  • • Reason for Refund Request
📌

Step 2

Our team will review your request within 3-5 business days.

📌

Step 3

If approved, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

3

Refund Exemptions

🔹

No refunds for promotional or discounted plans.

🔹

No refunds for expired or partially used subscriptions.

🔹

No refunds if your account was suspended for policy violations.

4

Subscription Cancellations

  • • You can cancel your subscription anytime in Account Settings.
  • • Cancellation ensures no future charges, but no refunds for past payments.
