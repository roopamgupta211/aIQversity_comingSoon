General Questions

aIQversity is an AI-powered learning platform that helps students prepare for exams using Generative AI (GenAI). We provide smart study plans, AI-based tutoring, question paper generation, doubt-solving, and real-time exam predictions.

aIQversity serves students at every educational level - from K-12 (all streams) through undergraduate, postgraduate, and higher studies. We support comprehensive competitive exam preparation including JEE, NEET, UPSC, and other government exams. Parents, teachers, and educational institutions can also leverage the platform to enhance and support student learning through AI-powered insights and tools.

Yes! aIQversity is available on desktop, mobile, and tablet. We will soon launch a dedicated mobile app for Android and iOS.

Yes! We provide learning support in English, Hindi, and other regional languages, with AI-powered translations and voice-based explanations.

Features & Functionality

Our AI analyzes your IQ level and adapts the learning content accordingly. The system automatically adjusts question difficulty levels based on your IQ assessment, ensuring optimal challenge and learning progression for each individual student.

Our Generative AI analyzes 20+ years of past exam papers and syllabus trends to create realistic, probable exam papers. It categorizes questions by topic, difficulty, and marks distribution.

Absolutely! Our AI provides comprehensive explanations for all subjects across K-12, undergraduate, postgraduate, and competitive exams. Students can ask for concept explanations, upload questions via text or images, and receive detailed step-by-step solutions with related learning concepts. The AI supports multiple languages and learning styles to ensure effective understanding.

AI analyzes your strengths, weaknesses, and available study time to create a customized study plan. It dynamically adjusts if you miss a day or need extra practice on certain topics.

We offer AI-based virtual tutoring that works 24/7. Students can ask questions via chat, voice, or video, and AI will respond in real-time.

Our AI uses historical data, syllabus changes, and weightage analysis to suggest likely exam questions. It also adapts predictions based on student performance.

AI-Powered Doubt Solving & Tutoring

Students can either type their questions directly or upload photos of their problems. Our AI uses OCR technology to read text from uploaded images, then analyzes the problem, explains step-by-step solutions, and provides related concepts for better understanding.

Unlike other platforms, aIQversity doesn't just provide final answers. Our AI guides students step by step, explaining the why and how behind every answer.
